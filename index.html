About Farmer Portal

The Farmer Management Portal is a comprehensive digital platform developed by Lovely Professional University to streamline agricultural operations and government scheme implementations for farmers across Punjab.

Our mission is to empower farmers with easy access to their records, government schemes, and agricultural resources through a user-friendly digital interface.

Our Vision

To create a digitally empowered farming community with transparent access to agricultural resources and government support.

Features

  • Farmer record management
  • Scheme application tracking
  • Agricultural resource center
